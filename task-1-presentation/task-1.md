I created my infographic using **Canva**, a popular and user-friendly design tool. Canva helped me easily arrange text, icons, and colors into a clean and visually appealing layout. 
The design focuses on three main areas of digital literacy. First, it explains what digital literacy is, highlighting skills like using technology, finding information online, and 
communicating responsibly. Second, it presents useful digital tools for students, such as note-taking apps, presentation software, and online learning platforms that make studying 
easier and more organized. Third, it includes important safe internet practices like protecting personal information, using strong passwords, and avoiding suspicious links.
The overall design uses bright colors, simple icons, and clear headings to make the information easy to understand at a glance. I tried to keep it engaging and not too text-heavy 
so that viewers can quickly learn key points. One thing I found slightly difficult was choosing the right layout and balancing the amount of text with visuals. However, it was also 
interesting to explore different design elements and see how they improve communication.
